niRvAG Support FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a 30-day return policy on all unused items in their original packaging. To initiate a return, contact our support team with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was damaged or defective.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within India. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business days delivery. International orders may take 10-15 business days depending on the destination country. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: You can track your order using the tracking number sent to your email. Visit our website and enter the order ID in the order tracking section. Alternatively, contact our support team with your order number and we will provide real-time tracking information.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and digital wallets including Paytm and PhonePe. For COD orders, there is an additional fee of Rs. 50. EMI options are available on orders above Rs. 3000.

Q: How do I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement if they have not been shipped yet. Go to your order history, select the order, and click Cancel Order. If the order has already shipped, you will need to initiate a return instead. Cancellation refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.

Q: What is your warranty policy?
A: All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Extended warranty options are available for purchase at checkout. Warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover damage from misuse, drops, or water exposure. To claim warranty, contact support with your proof of purchase.

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: You can reach our support team through our AI chat assistant on the website, by email at support@nirvag.com, or by phone at 1800-NIRVAG (1800-647824). Support hours are Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 9 PM IST. Emergency support for critical issues is available 24/7.

Q: Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?
A: Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders of 50 or more units. Contact our business sales team at business@nirvag.com with your requirements. Bulk orders typically receive 15-30% discount depending on the quantity and product category.
