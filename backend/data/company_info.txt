Return Policy
-------------
niRvAG offers a 30-day hassle-free return policy on all products:
- Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery for any reason
- Items must be in original packaging, unused, with all tags attached
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item
- Return shipping is FREE for defective or wrong items
- For change-of-mind returns, a flat ₹99 return fee applies
- Electronics have a 15-day replacement policy and 1-year manufacturer warranty
- Fashion items can be exchanged for a different size/color at no extra cost
- Perishable items and personalized products cannot be returned
